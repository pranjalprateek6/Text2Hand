# Text2Hand

Markdown becomes handwriting on ruled paper. Headings are centred and
underlined, because a pen has no bold.

## What it draws

- Nested lists indent one step per level
    - Like this deeper one

1. Numbered lists keep their numbers
2. And stay in order

> Quotes sit indented from the margin.

| Feature | Drawn as |
|---------|----------|
| Tables | a ruled grid |
| Figures | a captioned box |
